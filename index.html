Hi, I'm Gem!

My name is Gem, and I am a passionate and driven individual transitioning into the world of technology and computer science. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Behavioural Science (Psychology) from Queensland University of Technology, where I developed a deep understanding of human behavior, problem-solving, and critical thinking. Building on that foundation, I am now pursuing a Master of Information Technology with a specialisation in Computer Science at QUT, set to begin in February 2025.

Through this portfolio, I aim to showcase the projects and knowledge I gain throughout my journey. From foundational courses like Object-Oriented Programming and Data Structures to advanced topics such as Computational Complexity and Software Security, I am eager to apply my growing skills to solve real-world problems, create innovative solutions, and explore the intersection of technology and user experience.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and see my projects come to life as I grow in this exciting field. If you’d like to connect or collaborate, don’t hesitate to reach out!
